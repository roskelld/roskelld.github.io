The Beginning.

You enter the darkened room, piles of books and strange contraptions litter the space. The target sits hunched over a desk facing a set of glowing rectangles; he seems undisturbed by your arrival. Perhaps your stealthy entrance worked or maybe the man is too deep into his work to notice you.

Speak outLook Around

Knowledge.

After a moment you begin feeling confident enough to gaze around the room. Looking up you notice a selection of plaques. As you focus the runes start to look familiar. Perhaps interacting with them might gain you some knowledge on your target.

Conversation.

You go to speak but before any sound leaves your mouth you notice some symbols appear in front of you. You're not sure why but you suddenly feel like it might be better to try and communicate through one of them, which though you need to decide.
