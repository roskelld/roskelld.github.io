Dean Roskell

Concept. Build. Improve.
Worked on end-user facing applications since 1996. Got his start as a web application developer building and supporting database driven web platforms. In 2000 he transitioned into video game development, converting a hobby into a career. Designed games for LEGO, Warner Bros., and EA, across all major platforms including mobile. At the end of 2016, he left his Senior Designer position at BioWare to form PrimeVR, a company focused on building applications that make use of emerging technologies to solve modern problems.
